Our Vision

Our Vision is to build the most efficient projects on web & mobile, to organize mock interview and to provide fellowships & internships in order to help the community.

There are so many problems erupting all over the world. From world hunger to widespread epidemics, from terrorism to pollution, the list is endless.

So, we the UNICORN SIG is an IT Services, Mock Interviews & Research Paper Assistance Company. We are a team of Experienced & Young Professionals, who are trying to help community by developing commercial & social projects through web applications & mobile applications. We are trying to make this world a better place.
